Our History & Certifications

1974

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is first opened, establishing itself as a prominent venue for conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts.

1980

JCC hosts its first significant national conference, setting the stage for future important events.

1987

JCC achieves ISO 9001 certification for its quality management system, demonstrating its commitment to delivering high-quality services and customer satisfaction.

1992

JCC becomes the venue for the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference, a gathering of world leaders from non-aligned countries to discuss global issues and promote cooperation.

2000

The Asian-African Conference takes place at JCC, commemorating the historic 1955 Bandung Conference and bringing together leaders from Asia and Africa to foster collaboration and strengthen diplomatic ties.

2005

JCC undergoes renovations and upgrades to accommodate the increasing demand for world-class event facilities.

2010

JCC celebrates its 36th anniversary, having established itself as a prestigious venue with a rich history of hosting significant national and international events.

2015

JCC hosts a series of high-profile musical concerts, attracting renowned artists and entertainers from around the world, further cementing its reputation as a top destination for live performances.

2023

JCC continues its legacy of hosting important conferences, exhibitions, sports events, and concerts, remaining a preferred choice for both national and international gatherings.
